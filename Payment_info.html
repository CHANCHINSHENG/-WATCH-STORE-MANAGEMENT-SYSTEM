📄 Your Quick Payment Guide 💳💻🛍️

Hello there! Ready to complete your purchase? Here’s a simple guide to help you with our payment methods.

🚚 Important First Step: Your Shipping Information

No matter which payment method you pick, please start by accurately filling in all your Shipping Information. This helps us get your order to you!

You'll need to provide:

  • Full Name
  • Address
  • City
  • Postcode
  • State
  • Phone Number

Once this is done, you can choose how you'd like to pay!

1. Paying with Visa (Credit/Debit Card) 💳

If you select "Visa" as your payment method:

On our secure payment form, you will need to provide the following card details:

  • Card Number (16 digits): The long number on the front of your card.
  • MM (Expiry Month): The 2-digit month your card expires (e.g., 09 for September).
  • YY (Expiry Year): The 2-digit or 4-digit year your card expires (e.g., 25 or 2025).
  • CVV (3-4 digits): The 3 or 4-digit security code, usually on the back of your card.
  • Cardholder Name: The name written on your card.
  • Bank Name: The name of the bank that issued your card.

After entering these details, you will receive an order confirmation.The store will ship to you immediately

2. Paying with Online Banking (e.g., FPX) 🏦➡️💻

If you choose "Online Banking":

  1. First, you'll select your preferred bank from the list provided.
  2. You will then be securely redirected to your bank's official online login page. 🔒
  3. On your bank's page, you will be asked to log in and authorize the payment. For this, you'll typically need to enter your:
    • Account Number (Your online banking Account Number)
    • Password (Your online banking password)
    • Our websites support up to 9 types of bank to payments.
  4. Once the payment is authorized with your bank, please wait to be redirected back to our store for your order confirmation. Please don't close the window too soon! 🔄

3. Paying with Cash on Delivery (COD) 🛍️💵🤝

If you select "Cash on Delivery (COD)":

  • This is super easy! For COD, you only need to complete your Shipping Information accurately. 👍
  • No online payment details are needed on our website.
  • Simply confirm your order.
  • Our team will then prepare your items for delivery.
  • Please have the exact cash amount ready to pay our delivery rider when your awesome new items arrive at your doorstep! 💰
